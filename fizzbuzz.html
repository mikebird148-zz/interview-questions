Fizz Buzz

This is an exercise to practice JavaScript. Starting with 1 and ending with 100 we will print either the number, Fizz, Buzz or Fizz Buzz in the console.

For this exercise, for every number that is divisible by 3 with no remainder, the word Fizz will be printed instead of the number.

For this exercise, for every number that is divisible by 5 with no remainder, the word Buzz will be printed instead of the number.

For this exercise, for every number that is divisible by 3 and 5 with no remainder, the words Fizz Buzz will be printed instead of the number.
